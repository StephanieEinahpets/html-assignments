Fun Cat Facts

  1. Cats can rotate their ears 180 degrees.
  2. A group of cats is called a clowder.
  3. Cats sleep for 70% of their lives.
  4. They have five toes on their front paws and four on the back.
  5. Cats can't taste sweetness.

Cat Care Tips

Make sure your cat always has access to fresh water, nutritious food, and a clean litter box. Regular vet visits and lots of love are key to a healthy cat.
